FAQ

HAVE QUESTION?

+ How do I start searching for a car?

Simply start by selecting your preferred car type (SUV, Sedan, Hatchback, etc.), then filter your search based on criteria like brand, price range, fuel type, and other specifications. You can also compare multiple cars to see which one suits you best.

+ Can I compare cars on your platform?

Yes! Our platform allows you to compare multiple cars side-by-side, including specifications, features, pricing, and other important details. This helps you easily make a choice based on what fits your needs.

+ Do I need to register to use the platform?

No, registration is not required to search for cars or view their details. However, if you want to save your searches or receive personalized recommendations, you can create an account for free.

+ How accurate is the car information provided?

We provide up-to-date information sourced directly from car manufacturers, dealerships, and trusted car review sources. However, we recommend checking with dealers for the most current availability and prices.

+ How can I filter cars based on my budget?

You can set a price range filter to find cars within your budget. This filter allows you to select a minimum and maximum price to narrow down your search to only those cars that fit within your desired price range.

+ Can I find both new and used cars on the platform?

Yes, our platform offers both new and used cars. You can filter your search to display only new cars, used cars, or a combination of both.
